Educational visits


At Riget Zoo Adventures, we offer educational visits to anyone who wants to learn more about us and the animals that we have at our zoo.


Students can also take part in educational visits and we can accommodate up to 100 students for each educational visit. If the number of students reaches 100 on an educational visit, any additional students will be put on the next educational visit on a different day.


If you have any queries or are unsure about this, feel free to contact us (our contact details are on the contact us page).
